The Value of Water

An investigation into the capital value of water in farmland, centred on Fresno County, California.

This web page provides a web-based tool that estimates the economic value water adds to land using hedonic modeling and other regression methods. This project focused on Fresno County due to the heavy influence of agriculture in the region and the reliance on waterâ€™s economic benefits to the region.

Using this tool, landowners, urban planners, economists and policymakers can make data-driven decisions when assessing the impact water policy or infrastructure projects can bring to a region.
